I am a doctor in computer science, recently graduated from the University of Lille (Cristal / Inria). My work focuses on multi-objective optimization and more particularly on the decomposition of multi-objective problems.

I am currently a project holder at the Inria Startup Studio. The project consists in optimizing multiobjective urban mobility problems.

Current Interests and Projects:

  • Multiobjective optimization
  • MOEA/D algorithm
  • BigMO Project
